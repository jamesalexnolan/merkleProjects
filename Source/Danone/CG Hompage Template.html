Staying healthy in pregnancy

Pregnancy affects women in different ways, but however you cope with the demands of your emerging bump, make sure you take time to rest. Your healthcare professional will be accustomed to every kind of question so don't feel embarrassed to ask! Our articles offer useful help & advice too.

Weaning your baby

Weaning your baby is simply the process of slowly introducing solid foods to your little one’s diet to complement milk feeding. It’s an exciting part of your baby’s development and an essential one. At around six months your little one gradually begins to rely on solid food to get all of the nutrients they need to grow big and strong.
